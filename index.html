You have 27 points to spend on your ability scores.
The cost of each score is shown on the ability score Point Cost Table.
For example, a score of 14 costs 7 points.
Using this method, 15 is the highest ability score you can end up whit, before applying racial increases.
You can't have a score lower than 8.
